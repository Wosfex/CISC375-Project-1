About Me:

My name is Andrew Steichen, and I am a Comp. Sci. major at St. Thomas. I attended Mounds View High School and live north of the cities. I used to be a Mechanical Engineering major but switched after a bit.
The main reason I chose St. Thomas was because it is almost entirely taught by professors. My grandpa had attended the U for electrical engineering, and he had tons of stories; many of them involved TAs not caring about classes or generally being horrible teachers. Attempting to avoid that brought me to St. Thomas. My long-term goals involve getting a job, hiking a bunch, and camping a lot.
