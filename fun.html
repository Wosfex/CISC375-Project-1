My Dog:

Elly

Elly is a Shih Tzu and is 15 years old; she is doing well which is quite surprising as Shih Tzu's generally live to be 14.

Elly has been sweet and mellow all the time, just content to play or lay around. As one can see from the pictures, she is adorable. This is not up for debate.

Shih Tzus originate from China where they were favorites of Chinese royal families. They were so highly regarded that it wasn't until 1930 that a Shih Tzu was imported outside of China. The introduction of the Shih Tzu to the Americas was in the 50s when servicemen brought home these small dogs.


Ultimate Frisbee

Officially known as Ultimate due to trademark reasons, ultimate frisbee is a 7v7 game with a plastic disc and a decent amount of field space. I've played for a few years and the sport is quick and fluid.

The rules are pretty simple, but here are the basics: when you've got the disc, you have to stop; you've got 10 seconds to throw the disc or it's a turnover; and if the disc hits the ground it's a turnover.
